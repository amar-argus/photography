About

Argus in its last two years journey has emerged as a channel that champions the cause of poor and downtrodden in the state of Odisha. With tag line ‘In Search of Truth’ and ‘Argus always with the people’, the channel has reached out to the people in the remote part of the state which other established channels have even found difficult to reach. Journalism with courage has been the channel’s identity.
